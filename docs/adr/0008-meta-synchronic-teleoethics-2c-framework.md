# ADR 0008 — 2C$ Meta-Synchronic Teleoethics Framework

---

## Context

Airic Easm shared a document titled "2C$" on Meta-Synchronic Teleoethics applied to regenerative community design. The document proposes a self-sustaining BioArcology-EcoPermaculture model structured as an RV campground + co-op farm + bodega. The most novel element is the philosophical synthesis — toroidal dynamics, fractality, egregore theology, Timewave Zero — mapped onto concrete engineering and governance frameworks.

This ADR evaluates each philosophical element for use in RegenTribes architecture work.

---

## Decision

Adopt toroidal dynamics and fractality as grounded design patterns. Reject Timewave Zero as a planning assumption. Bridge egregore theology to the Field of Trust framework via measurable indicators.

---

## Toroidal Dynamics — APPROVED

**What it is:** A topology for self-sustaining resource loops. What comes in, circulates, exits cleanly.

**Why it works:** Already used in permaculture guilds, water cycling, and some DAO token flows. Grounded, observable, testable.

**Application:** Resource loop design for regenerative communities. Data loops. Energy loops. Governance feedback loops.

---

## Fractality — APPROVED

**What it is:** Self-similar structures at every scale. The same governance loop at household, village, bioregion.

**Why it works:** Connects to Ostrom's work on commons governance. Multi-scale commons design is well-documented.

**Application:** Governance recursion. The same accountability loop at every layer of the community structure.

---

## Timewave Zero — REJECTED

**What it is:** McKenna's novelty theory based on I Ching hexagram sequences.

**Why it fails:** Not falsifiable. Cannot be tested or surprised. A framework that cannot be wrong is a story, not a planning tool.

**Decision:** Treat as texture only. Do not use Timewave Zero as a planning assumption or decision input.

**Alternative:** Use observable, falsifiable acceleration metrics instead. Patent filings. Compute cost curves. Adoption rates.

---

## Egregore Theology — CONDITIONAL

**What it is:** Collective consciousness as a real emergent entity formed by shared belief and intention.

**Value:** Narrative identity for the collective. Something members can orient around.

**Risk:** Can become mythology masking extraction.

**Decision:** Accept as narrative layer. Connect to Field of Trust (Vic Desotelle's framework) which has measurable structure (5 indicators, hologram principle).

**Bridge:** Egregore = narrative identity. FOT = measurable indicators. Combine: measure what you narrate.

---

## Four Distinctions — REQUIRED ADOPTION

**What it is:** Vic Desotelle's framework separating Needs, Beliefs, Principles, Values — rigorously.

**Why it matters:** The 2C$ document conflates values and principles in places. This is a methodological gap.

**The four distinctions:**

| Distinction | What it captures |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Needs | What people require (survival, belonging, security) |
| Beliefs | What people think is true about the world |
| Principles | Why something matters — requires others to practice |
| Values | What people care about, in the abstract |

**Desert Island Test:** A value held but never practiced is just a preference. A principle is verified by behavior, especially under pressure.

**Required action:** Add Four Distinctions to DAO governance onboarding process.
