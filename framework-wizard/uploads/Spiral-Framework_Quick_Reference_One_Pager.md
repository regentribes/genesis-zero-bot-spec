The 5 Spirals of Community Development
SPIRAL 1: PROOF-OF-CONCEPT (Months 0-6)
Question: Is this vision possible?
Condition Sets to Validate:
✓ Core Team & Vision Coherence
5-7 founders aligned
Shared vision documented
Decision-making method chosen
✓ Market & Feasibility Exploration
20-30 interviews show demand
3-5 bioregions researched
Rough financial sketch
Gate 1 Decision: Proceed to land search?
Key Deliverable: Feasibility report + committed team
SPIRAL 2: FEASIBILITY VALIDATION (Months 3-12)
Question: Can this actually work HERE with THIS capital?
Condition Sets to Validate: 
3. ✓ Land Specificity
Property identified & assessed
Due diligence completed
Land under control (optioned/leased/purchased)
✓ Capital Pathway Clarity
Detailed capital model: $__________
Phased development planned
Funding sources identified
Gate 3 Decision: Commit to land + capital?
Key Deliverable: Land secured + capital strategy
SPIRAL 3: CAPITAL OPTIMIZATION (Months 9-15)
Question: Can we raise capital AND prepare to build?
Condition Sets to Validate: 
5. ✓ Team Expansion & Readiness
Architect, engineer, lawyer, CFO hired
Governance training completed
Team decision-making tested
✓ System Integration
Master plan complete
All 5 pillars designed as one system
Impact targets set
✓ Operational Readiness
Legal entities established
Operations manuals drafted
Early community activated
Gate 5 Decision: Ready to build?
Key Deliverable: Capital raised (70%+) + detailed design
SPIRAL 4: CONSTRUCTION & ACTIVATION (Months 12-36+)
Question: Can we build & occupy this place?
Condition Set to Validate: 
8. ✓ Physical Systems Operational
Phase 1 built
First residents moved in
Systems working in real conditions
Culture alive
Gate 6 Decision: Operational community confirmed?
Key Deliverable: Living regenerative neighborhood
SPIRAL 5: CONTINUOUS REGENERATION (Year 2+)
Question: Is this actually regenerating?
Condition Set to Validate: 
9. ✓ Regenerative Performance
Ecological impact measured & improving
Community thriving
Economically sustainable
Ready to replicate/expand
Gate 7 Decision: Model proven?
Key Deliverable: Documented learnings + framework contribution

Key Success Factors
1. Experienced Facilitator
Someone who's built community before
External perspective + local knowledge
Conflict resolution skills
2. Clear Governance
Decision-making method chosen early
Practiced before major decisions
Documented & adapted
3. Realistic Timelines
Land search typically 6-9 months
Don't force rushed decisions
Better to wait for right land than be stuck with wrong land
4. Continuous Feedback
After each gate, pause & learn
Adapt based on what you discover
Build in reflection cycles
5. Strong Market Validation
Know there's actual demand
Interview target members early
Don't guess on this
6. Early Culture Seeding
Don't wait for perfect conditions
Start community gatherings now
Values lived before occupancy
