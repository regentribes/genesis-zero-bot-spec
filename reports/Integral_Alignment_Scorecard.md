# INTEGRAL ALIGNMENT SCORECARD — 100 Opportunities

### Based on: Integral Whitepaper v0.1 + DevGuide v0.1 | Compiled: 2026-04-30

---

## ASSESSMENT FRAMEWORK

**The 5 Core Subsystems (from DevGuide):**
1. **CDS** — Collaborative Decision System: decisions by consensus, weighted by skill + tenure
2. **OAD** — Open Access Design: all designs shared freely, with attribution
3. **ITC** — Integral Time Credits: non-transferable, decaying, based on three-signal valuation
4. **COS** — Cooperative Organization System: production planned, coordinated, quality-assured
5. **FRS** — Feedback & Review System: continuous monitoring, adaptive learning

**Zero-Tradeoff Criteria (per DevGuide p.2):**
- Does NOT require money as a primary incentive
- Does NOT rely on competitive markets for distribution
- Does NOT create accumulation dynamics
- Does NOT require external authority or corporate structure
- Does NOT extract from community or ecology
- Directly feeds into one or more Integral subsystems
- Can be initiated NOW with current community

**Scoring:**
- ✅ **FULL ALIGNMENT** — Feeds multiple subsystems, no tradeoffs, startable immediately
- ⚠️ **PARTIAL** — Works with Integral but requires external monetary interface or has minor tradeoffs
- ❌ **GOAT** (Goes Against Integral) — Profit-maximizing, extractive, accumulation-enabling, or incompatible
